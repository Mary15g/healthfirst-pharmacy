Our Story

Built on trust,
driven by care

For over 25 years, HealthFirst Pharmacy has been a cornerstone of community healthcare in Nairobi — combining clinical expertise with genuine compassion.

Qualified Pharmacists: All our staff hold recognized pharmaceutical qualifications and undergo continuous professional development.
Safe & Accredited: Fully licensed by the Pharmacy & Poisons Board with strict quality controls across all products.
Community-Focused: We partner with local clinics and hospitals to ensure seamless, coordinated care for every patient.
Modern & Accessible: Digital prescription management, home delivery, and extended hours make healthcare easier for everyone.
